IMPORTANT PASSENGER INFORMATION:- PLEASE NOTE

  • Opting for VIKALP does not mean that confirmed berth will be provided to passengers in alternate train. It is subject to train and berth availability.
  • Once confirmed in Alternate train, Cancellation charges will be as per your berth/train status in alternate train. In this scheme, your boarding and terminating station might change to nearby cluster stations.
  • You can be transferred to any alternate train opted and departing between 30 minutes to 72 Hours from the scheduled departure of original train, in which you have booked.
  • Option of VIKALP scheme is available at later stage before charting also through booked ticket history link. Please check PNR status after charting.
